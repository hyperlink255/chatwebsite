How to apply for WhatsApp Green tick verification?

WhatsApp green tick is a badge that appears on your WhatsApp profile. You will only receive it once your business profile has been successfully verified by WhatsApp, verifying that you are an official business account.

Businesses that have Official Business Account (OBA) status on WhatsApp, get a green tick besides their business name. Green tick is visible in the display name.

Approval depends on multiple factors such as brand credibility, your following on social media, listing in external media and your domain ranking can influence your green tick approval.

To request for a green tick verification please contact our support team. This will normally take 2 to 3 business days to get the approval. Once your request for the green tick is approved, your number will automatically be displayed as your business name, to your users along with the green tick. If your application for the green tick is rejected, it does not impact your ability to use Interact or WhatsApp business API.
