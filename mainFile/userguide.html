FAQ

How does your WhatsApp business account work before your Facebook business page is verified?

After creating a WhatsApp business account, you`ll need to verify your Facebook business page . This may require documents such as a certificate of incorporation or a business license.

Trial Experience allows you to send free text messages to limited numbers.

Once you've uploaded official documents to verify your business, you'll have access to additional messaging options.

Test Message Types When you send test messages in your unverified WhatsApp business account, you can send the following types of messages

Notifications: One way messages, such as an order alert or a shopping confirmation. These messages will be limited to WhatsApp-defined templates, only during the unverified state to test the experience.

A conversation is a 24-hour messaging window with a given phone number.

Please note that this trial experience expires in 7 days. You are required to complete your business verification before your trial access expires in 7 days.

After your business is verified, you can send more messages.

How can I unlock unlimited messaging?

You have to start your Facebook business verification process right away. As soon as your business gets verified, the messaging restrictions will be lifted and you`ll be able to use unlimited access.

You can start your verification process by submitting the required details for Facebook verification.

After submitting all the required details, you will have to wait until your verification is approved, which usually takes 3 to 10 business days from Facebook’s end. You are required to complete your business verification before your trial access expires in 7 days.

How does pricing work?

There are two components to the pricing structure:

1. Subscription charges – Plan that you choose for your business. This is charged by Chat Sense.

2. WhatsApp conversation charges - All conversations between your business and the user.

Chat Sense total monthly fee = Subscription charges based on your plan + WhatsApp conversation charges
